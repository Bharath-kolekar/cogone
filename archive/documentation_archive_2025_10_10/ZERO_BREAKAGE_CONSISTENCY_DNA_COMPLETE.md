```python
def _calculate_breakage_risk(
    consistency_score: float,
    critical_issues: int,
    high_issues: int
) -> float:
    """
    Calculate breakage risk percentage
    
    Formula:
    - Base risk = (100 - consistency_score)
    - Critical issues: Each adds 10% risk
    - High issues: Each adds 5% risk
```
